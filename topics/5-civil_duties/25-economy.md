## Uitdrukkingen — Common phrases

| Nederlands | English | Example | ExampleEn |
|------------|---------|---------|---------|
| Hoeveel kost het? | How much does it cost? | **Hoeveel kost het** in totaal? | How much does it cost in total? |
| Dat is te duur | That is too expensive | Nee, **dat is te duur** voor mij. | No, that is too expensive for me. |
| Dat is goedkoop | That is cheap | Kijk, **dat is goedkoop**! | Look, that is cheap! |
| Kan ik pinnen? | Can I pay by card? | **Kan ik pinnen** bij de kassa? | Can I pay by card at the checkout? |
| Contant of met de kaart? | Cash or by card? | Betaalt u **contant of met de kaart**? | Are you paying cash or by card? |
| Geld uitgeven | To spend money | Ik wil minder **geld uitgeven**. | I want to spend less money. |
| Geld sparen | To save money | We proberen elke maand **geld te sparen**. | We try to save money every month. |
| Rondkomen | To make ends meet | Van dit inkomen kunnen we goed **rondkomen**. | We can make ends meet well on this income. |
| De rekening delen | To split the bill | Zullen we **de rekening delen**? | Shall we split the bill? |
| Op de kleintjes letten | To watch the pennies | Deze maand moeten we **op de kleintjes letten**. | This month we have to watch the pennies. |
