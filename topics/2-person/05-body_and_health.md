## Health

Words and phrases for talking about your health, the doctor and the pharmacy.

### Common phrases

| Nederlands | English | Example | ExampleEn |
|------------|---------|---------|---------|
| Hoe voel je je? | How do you feel? | **Hoe voel je je** nu? | How do you feel now? |
| Wat heb je? | What's wrong with you? | Je ziet bleek, **wat heb je**? | You look pale, what's wrong with you? |
| Ziek | Sick | Ik ben vandaag **ziek** en blijf thuis. | I'm sick today and staying home. |
| De gezondheid | The health | Een goede **gezondheid** is heel belangrijk. | Good health is very important. |
| Gezond | Healthy | Groente en fruit zijn **gezond**. | Vegetables and fruit are healthy. |
| Beter worden | To get better | Rust goed uit, dan word je snel **beter**. | Rest well, then you'll get better quickly. |
| Ik voel me niet goed/lekker | I don't feel well | **Ik voel me niet goed** vandaag. | I don't feel well today. |
| Beterschap! | Get well soon! | **Beterschap**, word snel beter! | Get well soon, feel better soon! |
