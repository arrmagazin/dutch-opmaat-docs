# Degree, quantity & focus  *(A2)*

## Focus & stance ‚Äî which part, how likely

These point at one element or colour the whole sentence.

| Dutch | English | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| `liefst` | preferably / most of all | Ik kom `liefst` morgen. (I'd rather come tomorrow.) |
