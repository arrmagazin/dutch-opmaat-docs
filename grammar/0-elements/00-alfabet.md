# Het Nederlandse Alfabet  *(A1)*

Het Nederlands gebruikt het Latijnse alfabet met 26 letters.

| Letter | NL naam | IPA (naam) | NL spelalfabet | NATO (international) |
|:------:|:--------|:-----------|:---------------|:---------------------|
| **A a** | aa | /aː/ | Anna / Anton | Alpha |
| **B b** | bee | /beː/ | Bernard | Bravo |
| **C c** | cee | /seː/ | Cornelis | Charlie |
| **D d** | dee | /deː/ *"hot"* | Dirk | Delta |
| **E e** | ee | /eː/ | Eduard | Echo |
| **F f** | ef | /ɛf/ | Ferdinand | Foxtrot |
| **G g** | gee | /ɣeː/ | Gerard | Golf |
| **H h** | haa | /ɦaː/ | Hendrik | Hotel |
| **I i** | ie | /i/ | Izaak | India |
| **J j** | jee | /jeː/ | Johan / Jacob | Juliett |
| **K k** | kaa | /kaː/ | Karel | Kilo |
| **L l** | el | /ɛl/ | Lodewijk | Lima |
| **M m** | em | /ɛm/ | Maria | Mike |
| **N n** | en | /ɛn/ | Nico | November |
| **O o** | oo | /oː/ | Otto | Oscar |
| **P p** | pee | /peː/ | Pieter | Papa |
| **Q q** | kuu | /ky/ | Quinten | Quebec |

> The last two columns are for **dictating aloud** — Dutch speakers often use ad-hoc word-spelling (*"D van Dirk"*) rather than the NATO alphabet, but both are common.

## Diakritische tekens (Diacritics)

Dutch itself uses very few diacritics — but they appear in loanwords and to disambiguate.

| Mark | NL naam | Function | Voorbeeld |
|:----:|:--------|:---------|:----------|
| **´** | accent aigu | stress / French loanwords | ééne *(one — vs *een* "a")*, café |
| **`** | accent grave | open vowel (French loans) | scène, blèren |
| **^** | accent circonflexe | French loans only | enquête, gêne |
| **¨** | trema | "split this vowel from the previous" | België, geïnteresseerd, ruïne, patiënt |
| **ç** | cedille | French loans | reçu |
| **ñ** | tilde | Spanish loans | mañana |
