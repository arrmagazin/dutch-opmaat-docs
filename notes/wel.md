Core function: **affirmative counter to a negation**. No exact English word — closest is stressed "do/does" or "actually".

| Use | Example | English |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Rebutting a negative (key!) | — Je hebt het niet gedaan. — Ik heb het **wel** gedaan! | — You didn't do it. — I **did** do it! |
| Softening / concession | Dat is **wel** waar. | That's actually true. |
| | Ik wil **wel**. | I don't mind. / Sure. |
| Contrast in a sentence | Bier heb ik niet, wijn **wel**. | No beer, but I do have wine. |

`wel` is essentially the "anti-*niet*" — it always pulls toward "yes" against an expected "no".
