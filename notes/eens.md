Particle softening a command, adding a **"-/once/sometime"** nuance.

| Use | Example | English |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Softening a command | Kom **eens** hier. | Come here (gently). |
| a sec | Luister **eens**. | Listen a sec. |
| "sometime / one day" | Je moet **eens** langskomen. | You should drop by sometime. |
| Fairy-tale opener | Er was **eens**... | Once upon a time... |

> often reduced to `'ns` / `es`

## `even` vs `eens`

`even` is about **brevity** ("not for long"); `eens` is about **softening the tone / vagueness** ("go on", "sometime").

| Dutch | English | Nuance |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Kom **`even`**. | Come for a moment. | emphasizes: briefly |
| Kom **eens**. | Come here (do). | emphasizes: softened request |
| Kom **eens `even`**. | Come over for just a sec. | both at once |
