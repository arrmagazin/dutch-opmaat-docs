Modal particle.

Core meaning: **"still / anyway / after all"** — contrast or gentle insistence.

| Use | Example | English |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Against expectation | Het is duur, maar ik koop het `toch`. | It's expensive, but I'll buy it anyway. |
| Seeking confirmation | Je komt `toch`? | You're coming, right? |
| Mild reproach / reminder | Dat had ik `toch` gezegd! | I did say that, you know! |

Roughly: *still / after all / right / anyway*, depending on context.
